Welcome

Hello! I’m Antonio Aguirre, a dedicated Ph.D. candidate specializing in Bayesian Statistics, Machine Learning, and Spatio-Temporal Modeling. My mission is to leverage statistical methods to solve real-world problems, ranging from environmental challenges to advanced forecasting models.

Take a journey through my research, teaching philosophy, and technical contributions. Let’s explore the intersection of data science, innovation, and impactful applications.

Research Interests

  • Bayesian Modeling for Spatio-Temporal Processes: Understanding complex systems with high-dimensional dependencies across space and time.
  • Dynamic Quantile Linear Models (DQLMs): Innovating scalable, real-time forecasting methods for quantile estimation under uncertainty.
  • Hydrology and Environmental Statistics: Developing predictive models to better understand and manage water resources and environmental phenomena.
